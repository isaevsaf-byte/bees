Our Story

Raised at 2,000 Metres
Above the Silk Road

The Tian Shan range — the "Celestial Mountains" — has sustained beekeeping traditions for centuries. Our apiaries sit high in the unpolluted valleys of Uzbekistan, where wildflower meadows bloom without herbicides and cold winters produce bees of exceptional resilience.

We specialise in Carpathian queen bees — a breed prized across Europe for gentleness, productivity, and low swarming tendency. Every queen is reared in our high-altitude facility and dispatched to UK buyers from April through to August.
